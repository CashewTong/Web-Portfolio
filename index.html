Robotics

Automated Toy Car

A robotic car built with an Arduino Uno, sonar sensor, servo motor, infrared sensor, motor driver board, Bluetooth module. The car is powered by two 3.7V batteries and its wheels are powered by four DC motors. With the sonar sensor connected to a servo motor, the car is able to scan a full 180 degree for obstacles ahead to avoid collision. As well, the infrared sensor allows the car to autonomously follow a dark coloured line. As well, the Arduino Uno is connected to a Bluetooth module that allows connectivity to a remote via Bluetooth.

Automation

Automated Claw

This is an aluminum servo motor claw. Using rivets, sheets of thin aluminum, hole punches, copper wire, a servo motor, sensor and an Arduino Uno. It uses a sonar radar to read distances from a flat surface. WIthin the Arduino Uno board, it was programmed to read distances from the sonar every half a second and as it surpases 0.5cm it was activate the servo motor to move 180 degrees. Once, the claw recognizes another 0.5cm distance gap from the sonar, it will turn 180 degrees in the opposite direction. The servo motor is connected to the claws with a stiff, copper wire to allow the claw to open and close to grab objects.
